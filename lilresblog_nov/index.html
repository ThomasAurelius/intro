Starting off

November 2018

Starting off, after over a decade away, I am taking a very DIY and low budget approach to this. 10k+ is way too much for a bootcamp IMO, so I'm determined to get there through mostly free resources. I plan to document and share my path, in the event anyone coming after me wants to do the same.

Key resources:

  • CodecademyPro. Monthly. $19.99/mo. I liked it's format, and it runs (mostly) on some older OS's I use, when I have downtime.
  • Codeanywhere. I didn't do too much research into IDE's before I chose this. Again, picked it because it works on all my devices.
  • LinkedIn Learning Premium. Free Month Trial. I like the content so far, running concurrent with codecademy, its covering the same material, just in another flavor. I usually hate video content, but I mostly just listen to the videos while commuting.
  • Github. I signed up. Don't have much of a use yet. More on this later.
  • Skillcrush. Ok, mostly this page to give me a roadmap. Skillcrush Road Map
